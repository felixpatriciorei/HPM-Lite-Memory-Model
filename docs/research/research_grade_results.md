## Core interpretation

The new figure suite supports this tighter story:

> HPM-Lite v2 is not primarily failing because retrieval cannot find the fact. Retrieval top-1 stays near-perfect in most long-context runs. The visible failure mode is writer/value quality, and the writer-supervision schedule strongly controls exact recall.

## Claim-safe results

* 4096-token tf600 is stable across 8 canonical Kaggle seeds.
* 8192-token tf600 remains high across 8 canonical Kaggle seeds.
* tf600 beats tf200 at 4096 and 8192 with large effect sizes.
* Retrieval top-1 remains high even where exact recall falls.

## Exploratory / not final

* 12288-token results are promising but low-n.
* Mixed PC + Kaggle rows are sensitivity checks, not primary claims.
* Regression and LOWESS figures are diagnostic, not causal proof.
* No full component ablation claim should be made until real ablation flags/runs exist.
